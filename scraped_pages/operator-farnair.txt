Farnair Switzerland is an airline based in Basel, Switzerland. It operates regular express parcel services from Switzerland to the main hubs in Europe. It also specialises in operations for humanitarian organisations, the oil industry and other special operations. Its main base is EuroAirport Basel-Mulhouse-Freiburg.
