Katrien Peeters has worked in the operational environment as assistant, TWR and ACC controller at Brussels Airport and Canac. Until recently, Katrien worked as Training and Human Performance Manager at Belgocontrol. She will shortly take up a position within the EUROCONTROL Maastricht unit training team.
