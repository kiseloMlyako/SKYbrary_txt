Transmile Air Services is a cargo airline based in Malaysia. It is an express freight and freighter charter company. The airline was established in November 1993. Transmile Air was designated a National Cargo Carrier by the Malaysian Transport Ministry in 1996. It has Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport  as a hub.
