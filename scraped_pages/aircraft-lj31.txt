Light turbofan corporate jet. In service since 1988. Improved development of Learjet 35/36, combined fuselage and powerplant of Learjet 35/36 with modern wings of Learjet 55/60 (deltafins below the T-tail). Models 31A/ER for extended range with higher take-off weight. Gates Learjet is now part of the Bombardier Canada Group.
