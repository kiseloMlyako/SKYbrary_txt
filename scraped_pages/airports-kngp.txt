Description
Naval Air Station Corpus Christi, also known as Truax Field, is a naval base located 10 km southeast of the central business district of Corpus Christi, in Nueces County, Texas.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and mild winters. Most rainfall occurs during summer and is often associated with thunderstorms and occasional tropical storms (hurricanes).
