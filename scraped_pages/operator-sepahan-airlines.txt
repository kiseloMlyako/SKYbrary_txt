Sepahan Airlines was an internal division of the Iran Aircraft Manufacturing Industrial Company (HESA). It was an airline based in Isfahan and operating (only) six locally assembled Antonov An-140 aircraft. Following a fatal accident involving one of its fleet on 10 August 2014 crash, all Iranian-assembled An-140s were grounded and the airline ceased operating.
