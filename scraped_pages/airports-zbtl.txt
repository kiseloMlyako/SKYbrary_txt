Description
Tongliao Airport is an airport in Tongliao, Inner Mongolia, People’s Republic of China.
Climatology
Moist continental mid-latitude climate with warm to cool summers and cold winters (Köppen climate classification Dwb). The average temperature of the warmest month is greater than 10°C (50°F), while the coldest month is less than -3°C (27°F). Winters are severe with snowstorms, strong winds, and bitter cold from Continental Polar or Arctic air masses. Average temperature of the coldest month is -3°C (27°F) or lower. Average temperature of the warmest month is greater than 10°C (50°F). Precipitation is equally spread across the year.
