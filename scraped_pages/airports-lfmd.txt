Description
Cannes - Mandelieu Airport is an airport serving the city of Cannes. It is located 5 km west of Cannes.
Climatology
Dry-summer subtropical ”Mditerranean" climate (Köppen climate classification Csa) - characterized by hot, dry summers and mild winters. During the summer, subtropical high pressure cells dominate, making rainfall unlikely except for the occasional thunderstorm. In the winter, periodic storms bring rain (snow at higher elevations).
