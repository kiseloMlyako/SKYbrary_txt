Description
The Brazilian Association of Airlines (Associação Brasileira das Empresas Aéreas or ABEAR) was created in 2012 by and for the main Brazilian airlines. Current members include ABAETÉ, Boeing, GOL, GOLLOG, LATAM, LATAM Cargo, RIMA, SIDERAL and VOEPASS.
More information is available in Portuguese on the ABEAR website.
