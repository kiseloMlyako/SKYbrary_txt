Description
Scatsta Airport is a privately operated facility on the Island of Unst in the Shetland Isles which serves the offshore oil and gas industry. It is 8.0 km southwest of Sullom Voe Oil Terminal and 39 km north-northwest of Lerwick. It is used by both fixed wing and rotary wing aircraft.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb) - Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter.
