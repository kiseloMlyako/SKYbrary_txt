Cath Bishop











  Cath Bishop






Cath Bishop competed at three Olympic Games in rowing, in Atlanta (1996), Sydney (2000) and Athens (2004), winning the World Championships in 2003 and Olympic Silver the following year. She also worked as a diplomat and now works as a business consultant, coach and speaker. ‘The Long Win: The Search for a Better Way to Succeed’ is published in October 2020.
