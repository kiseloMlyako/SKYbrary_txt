ADREP Taxonomy









This category is under development. This page contains information and guidance material associated with ICAO ADREP Taxonomy. The ADREP taxonomy is a set of terms used by ICAO to categorize aircraft accidents and incidents and allow safety trend analysis on these categories. The ADREP taxonomy is part of the ICAO accident reporting system (ADREP).
