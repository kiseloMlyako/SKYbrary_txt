Description
Sloulin Field International Airport is a public use airport located 3 km north of the central business district of Williston, a city in Williams County, North Dakota.
Climatology
A semi-arid steppe climate (Köppen climate classification BSk), with generally cold, dry winters and hot summers. Late spring and early summer is, on average, the wettest period of the year.
