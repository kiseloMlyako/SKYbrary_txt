Dr. Immanuel Barshi is a Senior Principal Investigator in the Human Systems Integration Division at NASA Ames Research Center. He studies the skilled performance of astronauts and pilots, mission controllers and air traffic controllers, their ability to manage challenging situations, and their vulnerability to error. He holds Airline Transport Pilot certi cate with A320, A330, B737, and CE500 Type Ratings, and is a certi ed ight instructor for airplanes and helicopters.
