Nicolas Suarez holds a B.S. in aerospace engineering and has a working experience of over 25 years in the Air Traffic Management (ATM) domain. His area of expertise is focused on the development, assessment and validation of ATM operational concepts and logical architectures. Currently, he is a technical manager in CRIDA (ATM R&D and innovation Reference Centre).
