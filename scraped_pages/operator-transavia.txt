Transavia (formerly Transavia Airlines CV and styled as transavia.com) is a Dutch based low-cost airline operating as an independent part of the Air France-KLM group. Its main base is at Amsterdam Airport Schiphol while Rotterdam The Hague Airport, Eindhoven Airport and Copenhagen Airport are its secondary bases. Transavia chiefly operates scheduled and charter services to leisure destinations. It is headquartered at Amsterdam Airport in Haarlemmermeer.
