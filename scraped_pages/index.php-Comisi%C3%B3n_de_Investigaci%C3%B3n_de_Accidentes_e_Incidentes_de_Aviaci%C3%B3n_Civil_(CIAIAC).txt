Definition
Comisión de Investigación de Accidentes e Incidentes de Aviación Civil (The Spanish Civil Aviation Accidents and Incidents Investigation Commission)
Description
CIAIAC is part of the General Transport Secretariat of the Spanish Ministry of Public Works, and is the official organization in charge of investigating civil aviation accidents and incidents that occur in Spanish territory.
