  Francois Jaulin






François Jaulin is a mathematician and a physician in anesthesiology, intensive care and aerospace medicine. Co-founder and President of the French Human Factors initiative, Facteurs Humains en Santé (gifh.eu), he has been interested in human error in medicine for several years. He is a partner and co-founder of the first independent reporting platform in perioperative medicine, Anesthesia Safety Network. François also provides in situ medical simulation training with EmergenSim.
