Large capacity long range airliner. The A330-800 is one of the versions of the A330neo (new engine option), the other being the A330-900. In service since 2020.
The A330neo offers new generation high-bypass ratio engines and improved wing aerodynamics. The A330-800 retains the fuselage length of the A330-200.
The Airbus A330-800 is part of the A330 aircraft family.
