Safety First
"Safety First" is published by the Flight Safety Department of Airbus.
It is a source of specialist safety information for the use of flight and ground crew members who fly and maintain Airbus aircraft. The magazine replaces the previously named “Hangar Flying”. It is usually issued twice a year, in January and July.
Airbus have implemented a dedicated website for Safety first, accessible at http://safetyfirst.airbus.com/
The website makes the content of the magazine available on a per article basis, enabling websearch by subject. It includes features that enable downloading articles individually, or download if images used in the article for own use.
