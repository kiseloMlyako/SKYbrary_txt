  Jerome Schimpff






Jérôme Schimpff started his career in 1998 as a first officer flying B737-200. Then he flew A310 and B747-200 before switching to the left seat in 2007. Now he is a Type Rating Instructor and Senior Examiner on A320. He obtained a Diploma in “Human Factors for the Conception of Human-Machine Systems” at the Paris V René Descartes University. He has been involved in CRM training for many years and is a member of the French Human Factor Reflection Group.
