Definition
Gabinete de Prevenção e Investigação de Acidentes com Aeronaves e de Acidentes Ferroviários - GPIAAF (Portugal)
Description
The former aviation AIA for Portugal the ‘GPIAA’ has been included in the new Portuguese Safety Investigation Authority (Gabinete de Prevenção e Investigação de Acidentes com Aeronaves e de Acidentes Ferroviários-GPIAAF) which conducts investigations into accidents involving all modes of transport.
