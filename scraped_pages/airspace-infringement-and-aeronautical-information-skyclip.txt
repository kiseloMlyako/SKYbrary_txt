Stijn De Graaff

Stijn de Graaff is an Operational Safety Specialist with the Safety Unit of the EUROCONTROL Network Manager Directorate, working there since December 2018. He has an operational background with the Royal Netherlands Air Force where he served for 13 years as a Tower and Approach controller, Flying Display Director and fulfilled Safety positions as Flight Safety Manager of the military ATC centre and Aircraft Accident Investigator.
