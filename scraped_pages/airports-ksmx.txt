Description
Santa Maria Public Airport, also known as the Capt. G. Allan Hancock Field, is an airport located 5 km south of the central business district of Santa Maria, a city in northern Santa Barbara County, California.
Climatology
Warm Mediterranean climate (Köppen climate classification Csb). Because of the airport's proximity to the ocean, onshore breezes significantly moderate temperatures, resulting in warmer winters and cooler summers compared with places farther inland.
