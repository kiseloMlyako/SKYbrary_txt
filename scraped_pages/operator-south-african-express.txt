South African Express Airways is an airline based in South Africa. Although the airline is operationally independent of South African Airways, its flights are incorporated within the strategic alliance with Airlink and South African Airways. South African Express started operations on 24 April 1994. The airline has its head office on the fourth floor of the West Wing of the Pier Development on the grounds of OR Tambo International Airport in Kempton Park, Ekurhuleni, Gauteng.
