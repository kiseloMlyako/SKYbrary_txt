The 2021 Safety Forum
Originally scheduled for June 2020, the event was held virtually on 11th and 12th February 2021. The event was dedicated to Airport Surface Risk.
The Forum was co-sponsored by the Flight Safety Foundation, European Regions Airline Association and EUROCONTROL.
