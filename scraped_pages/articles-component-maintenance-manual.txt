Definition
A formal document which details the way in which off-aircraft maintenance tasks on the specified component shall be accomplished. The maintenance tasks contained in these manuals do include procedures for restoring a structural component to a serviceable state and re-working and refinishing procedures are often provided in any appropriate CMM.
