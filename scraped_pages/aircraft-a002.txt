The Irkut A-002 is a three seat, pusher configuration autogyro developed in Russia through the 2000s by the United Constructor Bureau for Light Aircraft team of the Irkutsk Aircraft Production Association (IAPO) Irkut as the first independent product. The take-off from a place is possible when speed of a wind exceeds 8 m/s, otherwise running start up to 15 m is necessary.
