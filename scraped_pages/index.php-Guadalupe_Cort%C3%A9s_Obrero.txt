  Guadalupe Cortés Obrero






Guadalupe Cortés Obrero is a TMA controller at Barcelona ACC. She holds degrees in Linguistics, Air Transport Management, and Human Factors in Aviation. Guadalupe accredited as a Human Factors Specialist by the European Association for Aviation Psychology. She is co-founder of the Spanish professional air traffic controller association (APROCTA) and has been a member of IFATCA’s Professional and Legal Committee.
