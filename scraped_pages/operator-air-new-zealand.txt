Air New Zealand Limited is the national airline and flag carrier of New Zealand. Based in Auckland, New Zealand, the airline operates scheduled passenger flights to domestic destinations and international destinations in countries across Asia, Europe, North America and Oceania. Its hub is at Auckland Airport.
