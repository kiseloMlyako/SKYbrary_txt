Description
Corfu International Airport, "Ioannis Kapodistrias" or Ioannis Kapodistrias (Capodistrias) International Airport (IATA: CFU) is an airport on the Greek island of Corfu at Kerkyra, serving both scheduled and charter flights from European cities. The airport is located around 3 km south of Corfu Town, half a kilometre north of Pontikonisi.
Climatology
Dry-summer subtropical ”Mediterranean" climate (Köppen climate classification Csa) - characterized by hot, dry summers and mild winters. During the summer, subtropical high pressure cells dominate, making rainfall unlikely except for the occasional thunderstorm. In the winter, periodic storms bring rain (snow at higher elevations).
