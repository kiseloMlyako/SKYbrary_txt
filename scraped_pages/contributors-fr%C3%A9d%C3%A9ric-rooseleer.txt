  Frédéric Rooseleer






Frédéric has an ATM safety assessment background and is specialist on wake turbulence matters within EUROCONTROL. He led the development of the safety cases on RECAT-EU and Time-Based Separation solutions for optimised wake separation. He also represents EUROCONTROL at the ICAO Wake Turbulence Working Group (WTWG).
