  Sarah Sharples






Sarah Sharples is a Professor of Human Factors in the Faculty of Engineering at the University of Nottingham. She has led research in transport, manufacturing and healthcare, and currently leads the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) ‘Connected Everything Network Plus’. She was President of the Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors from 2015 to 2016. Sarah was recently appointed Chief Scientific Adviser for the UK Department for Transport.
