International airport serving Salalah. It is located on the Salalah coastal plain, 5.5 km northeast of Salalah's city centre.
Climatology
Hot Desert climate (Köppen climate classification BWh). Salalah is subjected to the south-west monsoons. This period (late June to early September) is known as the Khareef season. Visitors from across the Arabian Gulf States, mostly from the United Arab Emirates, and Saudi Arabia flock to Salalah to enjoy the monsoon and avoid the harsh heat faced by the rest of the region during the same period.
