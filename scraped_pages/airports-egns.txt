Description
Also formerly known as Ronaldsway Airport, the airport serves the whole of the Isle of Man, a self-governing British Crown Dependency.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing winds are south-westerly and whilst there is almost no risk of winter radiation fog or summer thunderstorms, the airport is exposed to a significant risk of advection fog between April and August.
