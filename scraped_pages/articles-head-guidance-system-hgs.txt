Description
HGS is an advanced head-up display providing essential flight information and guidance in all phases of flight such as:

recovery guidance in the event of unusual occurrences (TCAS alerts, unusual attitudes, wind shear)
flare guidance during final approach
low visibility take-off and approach guidance

For more information, see the separate article on Head up display (HUD).
