ICAO Member States and Related Territories
On this page you will find the states and related territories as defined in ICAO Doc 7910: Location Indicators, listed in alphabetical order.
Each directory entry includes a map of the state/territory and a list of airports located on that territory. This directory supports the display of SKYbrary accidents & incidents and airport articles in their geographical context.
