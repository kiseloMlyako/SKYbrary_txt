  Melanie Hulliger






Melanie Hulliger is a safety specialist in Zurich, Switzerland, who joined skyguide in 2013. With her degree in work and organisational psychology, her main focus lies on the development of a favourable safety culture. Next to her job at skyguide she also works as a Cabin Crew Member for Swiss Int. Air Lines and has a lectureship in Applied Psychology at the Zurich University of Applied Sciences.
