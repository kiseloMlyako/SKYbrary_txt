Background
This safety enhancement identifies or develops and implements methods for operators, regulators, and manufacturers to prioritize safety-related decisions. The project will improve methods of risk assessment for operational issues related to service bulletins, aircraft accident/incident analysis, flight-critical safety information, and recurring intermittent failures related to dispatch.
