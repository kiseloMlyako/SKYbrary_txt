Light aircraft. In service since 1979. Basic member of SOCATAs caribbean TB single engine aircraft series to replace the Rallye family. TB 10 Tobago and TB-200 Tobago XL are similar to TB-9 Tampico (TAMP) but with improved gear, higher weight and more powerful engine.
