Description
St. Petersburg–Clearwater International Airport is a public and military (USCG) use airport located in Pinellas County, Florida. It is 15 km north of the central business district of St. Petersburg and 11 km southeast of Clearwater.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Significant amounts of precipitation occur in all seasons in most areas.
