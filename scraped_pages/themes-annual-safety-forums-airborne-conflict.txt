The Airborne Conflict Safety Forum
On 10 June 2014 an Airborne Conflict Safety Forum was held at Eurocontrol in Brussels to achieve consensus on the way forward to reduce the risk of mid-air collision.
The event was co-hosted by Flight Safety Foundation, EUROCONTROL, and European Regions Airline Association, and held in Partnership with European Commercial Aviation Safety Team, International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), International Federation of Air Traffic Controllers' Associations (IFATCA), UK CAA, BEA (France), UK NATS, International Air Transport Association (IATA), ECA and DGAC (France).
