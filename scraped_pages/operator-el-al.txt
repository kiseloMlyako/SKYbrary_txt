El Al Israel Airlines Ltd, trading as El Al is the flag carrier of the State of Israel. It operates scheduled international passenger and cargo flights between its hub at Ben Gurion International Airport, serving Tel Aviv, and destinations in Africa, Asia, Europe, South America, and North America, as well as domestic connections to Eilat. Its head office is located on the grounds of Ben Gurion International Airport.
