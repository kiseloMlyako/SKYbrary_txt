The Swearingen Merlin or the Fairchild Aerospace Merlin is a pressurised, twin turboprop business aircraft first produced by Swearingen Aircraft, and later by Fairchild at a plant in San Antonio, Texas. In service since 1970. Development of SWEARINGEN Merlin 2 with a new tail. Improved version with winglets Fairchild 300. 92 Merlin 3 and 10 Merlin 300 were built.
