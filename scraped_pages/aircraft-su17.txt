Ground attack fighter. In service since 1970. Improved swing-wing development of Su-7. Built in many versions. Su-20 for export, Su-17R and Su-20R reconnaissance versions. Improved development Su-17M2 and shorter Su-17M2D since 1974. Improved export development Su-22. Su-17U and Su-22U two seat trainer versions. Su-17M3 and two seat Su-17UM3 with modified tail. In 2000 in wide spread use in Russia, Eastern Europe and the Middle east.
