The Go-Around Safety Forum
In June 2013 a Go-Around Safety Forum was held to achieve consensus on the way forward to improve go-around safety. The event was held in Partnership with European Commercial Aviation Safety Team, International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), International Federation of Air Traffic Controllers' Associations (IFATCA), UK CAA, BEA (France), UK NATS, International Air Transport Association (IATA), ECA and DGAC (France).
