Description
Tangshan Sannühe Airport, or Tangshan Air Base, is a dual-use military and civil airport serving the city of Tangshan in Hebei Province, China. It is located near the village of Sannühe, 20 km from the city center in Fengrun District. The airport was opened on 13 July 2010.
Climatology
Humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa.) Hot muggy summers with frequent thunderstorms. Winters are mild and precipitation during this season comes from mid-latitude cyclones. This climate type is quite common on the east coasts of continents.
