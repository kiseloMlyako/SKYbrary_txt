  Tom Young






Tom Young is a Performance Psychologist working in business and elite sport. He was recently part of the coaching team to PGA Tour and Ryder Cup golfer Tommy Fleetwood. He worked as a consultant to both the Belgian national team ahead of the World Cup in Russia, and the victorious European Ryder Cup team in Paris 2018. His book, ‘The Making of a Leader: What Elite Sport Can Teach Us About Leadership, Management and Performance’ is out now.
