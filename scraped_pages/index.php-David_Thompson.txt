  David Thompson






Dr David Thompson worked at NATS for several years working on new Air Traffic Control (ATC) procedures, airspace, workstations and HMI, and control rooms/towers. A highlight was providing the ergonomics and HF for the temporary airspace and revised ATC procedures for the 2012 Olympics. He now works freelance, providing HF expertise to clients in highhazard industries. He also lectures at King's College London on the Diploma of Aviation Medicine on the topics of Aviation Ergonomics, and Situation Awareness
