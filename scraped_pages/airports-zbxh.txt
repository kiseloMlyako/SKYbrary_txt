Description
Xilinhot Airport is an airport serving the city of Xilinhot in Inner Mongolia, China. It is located 9.5 km southwest from the city center. It has a single runway that is 2,800 meters long and 45 meters wide (class 4C).
Climatology
Dry arid climate common in middle latitude deserts (Koppen climate classification BWk). Annual average temperature is below 18°C (64°F). Daily minimum temperatures can frequently go below 0°C (32°F) during the winter season.
