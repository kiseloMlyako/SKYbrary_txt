Providenciales International Airport on the island of Providenciales is the main international airport serving the Turks and Caicos Islands, an overseas territory of the United Kingdom. The territory's other international airport is JAGS McCartney International Airport on Grand Turk Island.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification Aw) - monthly mean temperature above 18°C in every month of the year and a pronounced dry season.
The dry season is in Feb/Mar. The Hurricane season lasts from May to December with the rainy season extending into January.
