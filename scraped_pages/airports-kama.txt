Description
Rick Husband Amarillo International Airport is a public use airport located 10 km east of the central business district of Amarillo, in Potter and Randall Counties, Texas.
Climatology
Semi-arid Steppe climate (Köppen climate classification BSk) - Little precipitation, hot summers and cool, occasionally cold, winters, sometimes with large changes in temperature in a 24 hour period.
