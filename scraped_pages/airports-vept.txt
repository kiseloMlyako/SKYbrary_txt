Description
Lok Nayak Jayaprakash Airport, also known as Patna Airport and Jaiprakash Narayan Airport, Patna, is a public use airport located 5 km southwest of the central business district of Patna, the capital city of state of Bihar, India.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters.
