Ljubljana Jože Pučnik Airport (IATA: LJU, ICAO: LJLJ), also known by its previous name Brnik Airport, is the international airport of Ljubljana, Slovenia. The airport is located near the village of Brnik, 19 km north of Ljubljana and 11 km south of Kranj on the road between Kranj and Mengeš.
