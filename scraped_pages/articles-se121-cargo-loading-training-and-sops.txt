Background
This safety enhancement reduces cargo-related accidents and incidents by:

Publishing and enforcing clear, concise and accurate standard operating procedures (SOPs);
Training the rationale behind those procedures;
Ensuring company training programs are approved and monitored; and
Ensuring adequacy of contractor training.
