  René van Paassen






Assoc. Prof. René van Paassen, PhD, is an associate professor in Aerospace Engineering at Delft University of Technology, working on human machine interaction and aircraft simulation. His work on human- machine interaction ranges from studies of perceptual processes, haptics and haptic interfaces and human manual control, to the design of and interaction with complex cognitive systems, applying cognitive systems engineering and ecological interface design for vehicle control.
