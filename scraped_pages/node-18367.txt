Jet2.com Limited, also known simply as Jet2, is a British low-cost airline based at Leeds Bradford Airport, England. The airline also offers contract charter and air cargo services. Its main base and headquarters is at Leeds Bradford Airport.
The airline began operating in 1983 under the name Channel Express.
