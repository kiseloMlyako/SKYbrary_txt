Marcello Scala











  Marcello Scala






Marcello Scala has been in service as an air traffic controller at Milan ACC for ENAV since 2005. He was previously an officer in the Italian air force for fifteen years, where he performed the functions of ATCO with ADI- APS-ACS ratings, OJTI Licence endorsements and supervisor of operations room. In 2002, he graduated in Law, with his thesis on air navigation law.
