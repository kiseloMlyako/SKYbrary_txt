  Thomas Hellwig






Dr Thomas Hellwig works as a professor, programme director and coach for INSEAD in the field of organisational behaviour. He is a trained medical doctor with 20 years of experience (emergency, cardiology, and cancer medicine) in hospitals in three European countries. He has a doctorate in psychotherapy and an MBA from INSEAD. His special interests are in the areas of executive teams, leading change, organisational culture, and health and stress.
