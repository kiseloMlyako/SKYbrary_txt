  Barry Kirwan






Dr Barry Kirwan worked in Human Factors in the nuclear, oil and gas, and maritime sectors, and lectured at Birmingham University for five years before becoming Head of Human Factors at NATS. Joining EUROCONTROL in 2000, he initiated and ran the European Safety Culture Programme for a decade before moving onto European research projects focusing on Human Factors, safety culture and artificial intelligence applications.
