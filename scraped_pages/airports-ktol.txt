Description
Toledo Express Airport is a joint civil-military airport located 16 km west of the city of Toledo in the extreme Western portion of Lucas County, Ohio, United States. In addition to serving commercial passenger, cargo and general aviation aircraft the airport is also a base for the Ohio Air National Guard.
