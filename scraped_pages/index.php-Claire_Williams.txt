  Claire Williams






Dr Claire Williams is Technical Director of human sciences for Intelligent Mobility and Smart Technologies (IMST) in Atkins. Claire brings 22 years of Human Factors/Ergonomics combined consultancy and academic experience. Within IMST Claire is supporting Atkins to marry academic technical excellence and consultancy. She is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors, Visiting Research Fellow at the University of Derby and Honorary Associate Professor at the University of Nottingham.
