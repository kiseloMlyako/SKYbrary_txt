Description
Bird Strike Committee U.S.A. (BSC USA) was founded as a forum to facilitate the exchange of information and to promote the collection and analysis of accurate wildlife strike data, as well as the development of new technologies for reducing wildlife hazards and professionalism in wildlife management programs. It also serves as the national expert body and liaison to the U.S. Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), national and international bird strike committees and to other professional aviation and wildlife organizartions. 
BSC USA was founded in 1991. It is a volunteer organization directed by a steering committee comprising members from representative organizations, including FAA, the U.S. Departments of Defense and Agriculture, airlines, airports and other aviation industry stakeholders and the private sector.
