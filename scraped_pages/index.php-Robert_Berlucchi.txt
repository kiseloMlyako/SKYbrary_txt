Robert Berlucchi











  Robert Berlucchi






Robert Berlucchi is currently the Air Traffic Manager at West Palm Beach and works on the Airport Construction Advisory Council as a collateral duty. He began working for the FAA in 1979 and has 35 years of Air Traffic Control experience with the FAA. Robert was a US Navy controller for 4 years at NAS Whiting Field and aboard the USS Coral Sea. He joined the Airport Construction Advisory Council in 2010 and currently is a Co-Chairman of the ACAC.
