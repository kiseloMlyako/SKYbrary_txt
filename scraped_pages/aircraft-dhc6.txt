STOL turboprop airliner and utility transport aircraft. In service since 1966 (-200 since 1968, -300 since 1969). Fixed undercariage. DE HAVILLAND CANADAs most successful commercial design. DHC-6-200 with extended nose and DHC-6-300 with more powerful engines. Production ceased in 1988. DHC-6-300: Length 15,77 m MTOW 5,670kg.Production of a new series of the aircraft (400) started in 2007.
