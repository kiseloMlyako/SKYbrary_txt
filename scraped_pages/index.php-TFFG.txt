L'Espérance Airport, also known as Grand Case Airport, is a public use airport located in Grand Case, on the French side of the Caribbean island of Saint Martin. The airport is only used for smaller aircraft as Princess Juliana International Airport on the Dutch side of the island (known as Sint Maarten) is served by all major carriers.
