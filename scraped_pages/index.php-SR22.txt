The Cirrus SR22 is a single-engine, originally four and later five-seat, composite aircraft, built by Cirrus Aircraft starting in 2001. It is a more powerful version of the Cirrus CIRRUS SR-20, with a larger wing, higher fuel capacity, and a has fixed (non-retractable) landing gear.
