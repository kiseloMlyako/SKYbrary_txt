Description
Belfast City Airport, recently given the commercial identity 'George Best Belfast City' is a downtown airport in Belfast, County Down, Northern Ireland with a single runway and is situated adjacent to the Port of Belfast approximately 3 km from Belfast City Centre. It shares the site with the Bombardier (formerly Short Bros) aircraft manufacturing facility.
