Description
Durham Tees Valley Airport is a small international airport located near Darlington, North East England. About 16 km southwest of Middlesbrough and 39 km south of Durham. The airport serves County Durham, Teesside and parts of North Yorkshire. The airport is still widely recognised by its previous name, Teesside International Airport.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
