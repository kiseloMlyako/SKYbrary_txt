Description
The Flight Safety Foundation (FSF) European Aviation Safety Seminar (EASS), was held annually at various locations in Europe, until 2013 when the event was superseded by the Safety Forum.
EASS was co-presented by the European Regions Airline Association (ERA) and EUROCONTROL.
