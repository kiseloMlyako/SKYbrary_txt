  Marion Venus






Marion Venus started her career as work, clinical and health psychologist. After 10 years as health and safety manager in industry and aviation, she completed her PPL(A) in 2011. She started her PhD at the Department of Psychology of the University of Bern in 2017, entitled ‘Pilots’ working conditions, rosters, stress, sleep problems and fatigue, and how they can affect health and wellbeing’. Marion Venus is head of research and training at Venus Aviation.
