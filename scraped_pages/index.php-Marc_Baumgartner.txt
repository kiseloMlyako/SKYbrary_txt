Marc Baumgartner











  Marc Baumgartner






Marc Baumgartner is an air traffic controller and supervisor in Geneva ACC. Marc was a member of the Performance Review Body and the Chairman of the Performance Review Commission. For eight years until 2010, he was President and CEO of IFATCA, currently he coordinates the activities of IFATCA in SESAR and EASA.
