Turin-Aeritalia Airport, also known as Edoardo Agnelli Airport, is the historical airport of Turin in northern Italy. Torino-Aeritalia is used for tourist flights and as a flying school, both for gliding and powered flight. There is also a helipad for the use of air ambulances.
