Description
Macau International Airport situated at the eastern end of Taipa island and neighbouring waters, is the only airport in Macau.
Climatology
Macau has a humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa), with average relative humidity between 75% and 90%. Similar to much of South China, seasonal climate is greatly influenced by the monsoons, and differences in temperature and humidity between summer and winter are noticeable, though not as great as inland China. The average annual temperature of Macau is 22.7 °C. July is the warmest month, with average temperature being 28.9 °C. The coolest month is January, with a mean temperature of 15.0 °C.
