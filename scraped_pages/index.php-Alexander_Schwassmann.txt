  Alexander Schwassmann






Alexander Schwassmann is an operational air traffic controller with 25 years of TWR, APP and ACC experience. On behalf of IFATCA, he is a member of the ICAO ATM Requirements and Performance Panel, and a member of the EASA ATM/ ANS Technical Committee. He also holds ratings for gliders and single engine piston aircraft.
