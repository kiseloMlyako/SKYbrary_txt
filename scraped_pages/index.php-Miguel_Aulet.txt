  Miguel Aulet






Miguel Aulet is an ATCO at NATS Prestwick Centre. With a background in Industrial Design Engineering, he pursued an MSc in Human- Computer Interaction with Ergonomics at UCL. The Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors awarded his thesis ‘Cognitive Offloading in Air Traffic Control’ the ‘Ulf Aberg’ award for best post- graduate thesis. Miguel has collaborated in several HMI projects at NATS.
