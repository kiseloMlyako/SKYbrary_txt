Description
FlightAnalyst integrates tools necessary to load, store, pre-process, analyse, visualise, report, archive, maintain and animate single-flight and multi-flight aircraft or simulator data files. It can be used to analyse routine and special events, categorical events, exceedances, negative safety trends, and other flight training, operational or tactical issues. The results are presented to the end-users in a variety of charts and reports.
FlightAnalyst was developed by SimAuthor.
