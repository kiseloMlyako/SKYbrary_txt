Description
The NATO/EUROCONTROL ATM Security Coordinating Group (NEASCOG), was established jointly by the two organisations, and is the focal point for ATM Security in Europe covering both civil and military interests. NEASCOG acts as a forum for all national and international stakeholders that have a role to play with respect to airspace and ATM Security in Europe (notably International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO), International Air Transport Association (IATA) and International Federation of AirLine Pilots Associations (IFALPA)).
