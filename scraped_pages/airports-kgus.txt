Grissom Air Reserve Base, formerly Grissom Air Force Base, is a United States Air Force installation, located approximately 12 nm north of Kokomo, Indiana, USA. The facility is a joint use civil airport/military base shared with the Grissom Aeroplex which provides general aviation and charter service.
