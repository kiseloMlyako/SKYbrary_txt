Shuttle America Corporation is a regional airline based in Indianapolis, Indiana, USA. It feeds United Airlines flights at Chicago, Denver, and Washington/Dulles as United Express, and feeds Delta Air Lines flights at Atlanta and New York/LaGuardia as Delta Connection and Delta Shuttle.
