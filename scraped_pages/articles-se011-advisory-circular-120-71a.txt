AC120-71A
Advisory Circular AC120-71A (Standard Operating Procedures for Flight Deck Crewmembers) presents background, basic concepts, and philosophy in respect to SOPs. It emphasizes that SOPs should be clear, comprehensive, and readily available in the manuals used by flight deck crewmembers.
