Description
Arcata Airport, also known as Arcata-Eureka Airport, is an airport located 15 nm north of Eureka in the unincorporated town of McKinleyville, California. This regional airport serves Humboldt County, including the two primary regional cities that compose its name: Arcata and Eureka.
