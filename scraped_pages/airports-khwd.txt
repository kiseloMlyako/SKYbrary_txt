Hayward Executive Airport is a city owned, public use airport located two nautical miles west of the central business district of Hayward, a city in Alameda County, California, United States. This general aviation towered airport is situated near the eastern shore of San Francisco Bay. It was formerly known as Hayward Air Terminal.
