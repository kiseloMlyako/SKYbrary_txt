Description
Sri Guru Ram Das Jee International Airport is an international airport located 11 km northwest of the city of Amritsar, in the state of Punjab, India.
Climatology
Hot, semi-arid climate (Köppen climate classification BSh) - hot, sometimes extremely hot summers and mild to warm winters, with low annual precipitation, most of which occurs during the monsoon season (Jul-Sep).
