Light aircraft. In service since 1979. More powerful turbocharged development of retractable PA-28R-200 Arrow 3 with T-tail. Production ceased in 1982, resumed again in 1989 and ceased once more in 1992. Licence production by AICSA, Columbia, and CHINCUL, Argentinia, also by EMBRAER and NAIWA, Brazil, as EMB-711T/ST Carisco, Carisco Turbo.
