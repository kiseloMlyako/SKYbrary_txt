Bram Couteaux is a Boeing 737 First Officer with Transavia. Next to flying, he works in the safety department consulting operational departments on all matters safety-related and conducting incident investigations. He recently graduated from Lund University's Human Factors and System Safety MSc programme. This article is based on his master's thesis (Couteaux, 2023). Bram also is an avid glider pilot and flight instructor.
