Iran Aseman Airlines is an airline based in Tehran, Iran, Operates air taxi services and scheduled domestic and regional flights to the Persian Gulf area, as well as charter services. Its main bases are Tehran Imam Khomeini International Airport, Mehrabad International Airport, Dubai International Airport, with hubs at Shiraz International Airport and Mashhad International Airport.
