Corendon Dutch Airlines is a Dutch branch of the Corendon Group (which also operates Corendon Airlines in Turkey) which started operations under its own AOC in April 2011 serving European holiday destinations from Amsterdam Airport Schiphol. Its head office is in Lijnden, Haarlemmermeer.
