Description
Kapalua Airport, also known as Kapalua–West Maui Airport, is a regional private use airport on the west side of the island of Maui in Hawaii. It is located 9 km northwest of Lahaina, in Maui County, Hawaii.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification As) - monthly mean temperature above 18°C in every month of the year and a pronounced dry season.
