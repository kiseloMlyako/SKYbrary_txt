Norwegian Air International was an Irish airline and fully integrated subsidiary of Norwegian Air Shuttle. It operated flights to destinations in Europe and the Middle East and was headquartered in Dublin. It's aircraft and operations were taken over by its Swedish sister airline Norwegian Air Sweden in April 2021.
