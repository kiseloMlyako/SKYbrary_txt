Improved multi role fighter developments Su-27M (redesignated to Su-35) and thrust vectoring Su-37 with fly-by-wire flight control system. Highly agile aircraft used by the Russian, Belarus and Ukainean Air Forces in Europe, by the Chineese, Indian and Vietnamese Air Forces in Asia and Ethiopean Air Force in Africa.
