  Georgi Peev






Georgi has a master’s degree in engineering from the Technical University – Sofia specialising "Operation of electronic aviation equipment (Air Traffic Controller)". He became a professional air traffic controller in 2000 and has extensive experience in the field of civil aviation and air traffic management. In 2014 he was appointed Director General of BULATSA and participates in the governing bodies of a number of international organisations in the field of civil aviation.
