PrivatAir is an airline headquartered at Geneva International Airport in Meyrin, Switzerland, near Geneva. PrivatAir specializes in scheduled and charter flights using mid-sized, long-range, all premium class jets. One of the services it provides, is operating exclusively business class flights which include transatlantic operations for Lufthansa, Swiss International Air Lines, KLM and, until March 2008, corporate shuttles for Airbus.
