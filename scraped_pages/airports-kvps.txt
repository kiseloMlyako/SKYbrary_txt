Description
Northwest Florida Regional Airport is a joint use civil/military airport located within Eglin Air Force Base, near Valparaiso and Fort Walton Beach in Okaloosa County, Florida. Civil use is restricted to commercial traffic only.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Significant amounts of precipitation occur in all seasons in most areas.
