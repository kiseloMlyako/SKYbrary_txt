Description
Paris - Le Bourget Airport is an airport located in Le Bourget, Bonneuil-en-France, and Dugny, 6 nm north-northeast (NNE) of Paris, France. It is now used only for general aviation (business jets) as well as air shows.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
