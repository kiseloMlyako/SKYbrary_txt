Description
Førde Airport, Bringeland is a regional airport located at Bringelandsåsen in Gaular, 16 kilometers south of Førde, in Sogn og Fjordane county, Norway.
Climatology
Borderline Subpolar Oceanic/Continental subarctic climate (because of winter temperatures) (Köppen climate classification Cfc/Dfc) - uniform precipitation distribution, temperatures are moderated by the proximity of the ocean and warm currents - cool summers and comparatively mild winters.
