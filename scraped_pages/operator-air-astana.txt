Air Astana is the flag carrier of Kazakhstan, based in Almaty. It operates scheduled, domestic and international services on routes from its main hub, Almaty International Airport, and from its secondary hub, Nursultan Nazarbayev International Airport. It is a joint venture between Kazakhstan's sovereign wealth fund Samruk-Kazyna (51%), and BAE Systems PLC (49%)
