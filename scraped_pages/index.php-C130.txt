  C130






 

 





Military tactical and multi-role transporter with civil cargo versions. In service since 1955. Exists in many variants  both civil and military. The L-100/382 is a stretched civil version. Performance figures are for the C-130H. The C-130J is a modernised military version with new engines and electronics.
