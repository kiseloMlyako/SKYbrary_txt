Description
Rickenbacker International Airport is a joint civil-military (ANG) public use airport located 16 km south of the central business district of Columbus, in Franklin County, Ohio.
Climatology
Transition between humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) and humid "continental" climate (Köppen climate classification Dfa) - characterized by hot, muggy summers and cold, dry winters. Precipitation reaches its lowest in January and February and peaks in summer and latter half of spring. Winter snowfall is associated with large storms that the westerlies steer from west to east. Most summer rainfall occurs during thunderstorms.
