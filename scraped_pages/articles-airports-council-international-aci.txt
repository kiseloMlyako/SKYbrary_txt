Description
“Airports Council International is the association of the world's airports. It has 573 members operating 1751 airports in 174 countries and territories around the world. Established in 1991, ACI represents airports interests with Governments and international organizations such as ICAO, develops standards, policies and recommended practices for airports, and provides information and training opportunities to raise standards around the world.” source: ACI website
