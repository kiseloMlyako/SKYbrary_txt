Description
Gwalior Airport is a civil enclave airport at the Maharajpur Air Force Station located 10 km north-east of Gwalior, a city in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Almost all of the annual precipitation falls during monsoon season between June and October.
