Description
Bagdogra Airport or Civil Aerodrome Bagdogra is a civil enclave on a military airport that is located in the town of Bagdogra, 16 km west of the city of Siliguri, in the state of West Bengal, India.
Climatology
Humid Sub-tropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - Hot humid summers and cooler winter. Significant precipitation in all seasons but drier in the winter.
