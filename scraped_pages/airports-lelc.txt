Murcia–San Javier Airport is a military air base and former civilian passenger airport located in San Javier, 42 km southeast of Murcia, Spain. It is owned by the Spanish Air Force. It was replaced (after several delays) by the new Región de Murcia International Airport, when it opened on 15 January 2019.
