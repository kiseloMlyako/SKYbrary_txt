Description
Provo Municipal Airport is a public use airport located 3 km west of the central business district of Provo, a city in Utah County, Utah.
Climatology
Dry-summer continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dsa) a relatively rare form of the continental climate where a region experiences dry summers and wet winters. There are four distinct seasons. Both summer and winter are long, with hot, dry summers and cold, snowy winters separated by brief spring and fall transition periods.
