Formerly known as Chinggis Khaan International airport, Buyant-Ukhaa International Airport (from 2005 to 2020) serves Ulaanbaatar, capital of Mongolia. It is located 11 nm southwest of the city.
The airport serves as backup to the new Chinggis Khaan airport (ZMCK)
