  Paul Reuter






Paul Reuter is a Captain Boeing 737NG for Luxair, and President of the European Pilot Peer Support Initiative. He is a former president of Luxembourg's Airline Pilot Association, former Technical Director of the European Cockpit Association, and former Chairman of ECA's Safety Strategy Task Force. Paul is an IFALPA Accredited Accident Investigator.
