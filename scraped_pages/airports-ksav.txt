Description
Savannah-Hilton Head International Airport is a public and military (ANG) use airport located 13 km northwest of the central business district of Savannah, a city in Chatham County, Georgia. It has been formerly known as Savannah International Airport, Travis Field and Chatham Field.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Significant amounts of precipitation occur in all seasons in most areas.
