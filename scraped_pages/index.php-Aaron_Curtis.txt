  Aaron Curtis






Aaron Curtis is Vice Chair of the European Transport Workers’ Federation (ETF) Air Traffic Management Committee, and Lead representative for International and Governmental affairs for Prospect ATCOs’ Branch (the union representing Air Traffic Controllers in the UK). He is an operational air traffic controller (ATCO) at the London En Route Centre at NATS’ Swanwick facility, a role he has held for the last 16 years.
