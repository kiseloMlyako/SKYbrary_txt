Scandinavian Airlines or SAS, previously Scandinavian Airlines System, is the flag carrier of Denmark, Norway and Sweden. It is part of the SAS Group and headquartered in the Scandinavian Airlines head office in Solna, Sweden. The airline's main hubs are Copenhagen Airport, which is the main European and intercontinental hub, Stockholm-Arlanda Airport and Oslo Airport, Gardermoen. It also operates related subsidiaries including SAS Ground Handling (SGH).
