Montserrat Airways Ltd (MA LTD), trading as Fly Montserrat, is an airline with its headquarters at John A. Osborne Airport in Gerald's, Montserrat, British West Indies. The airline flies from Montserrat to and from Antigua, Barbuda and Nevis with Britten Norman BN-2 aircraft.
