Description
Pointe-à-Pitre International Airport or Pointe-à-Pitre Le Raizet Airport or Aéroport Guadeloupe Pôle Caraïbes is an airport serving Pointe-à-Pitre on the island of Grande-Terre in Guadeloupe. The airport is located in Abymes, 2.4 km north-northeast of Pointe-à-Pitre.
