  Pete McCarthy






Pete McCarthy is Head of Group Human Factors at Cathay Pacific Airways. He is also Visiting Fellow (Human Factors and Accident Investigation) at Cranfield University, and has acted as aircraft accident investigator in the British Army, Military Air Accident Investigation Branch, Farnborough, UK. He is undertaking a PhD in aviation safety and human factors at Cranfield University.
