Objectif SECURITE
Objectif SECURITE is a safety bulletin published by DSAC, the Civil Aviation Safety Directorate of the French DGAC. It is one of the safety promotion tools implemented in the framework of the State Safety Programme (SSP) of France.
This bulletin adresses safety issues impacting either commercial or general aviation, or both activities. It not only targets pilots but also air traffic controlers, ground service agents, airport personnel, etc. Its main objective is to promote safety and a safe attitude.
