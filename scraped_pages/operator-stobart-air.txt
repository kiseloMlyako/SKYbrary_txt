Stobart Air, legally incorporated as Stobart Air Unlimited Company is an Irish regional airline headquartered in Dublin. It operates scheduled services under the brands Aer Lingus Regional, Flybe, BA CityFlyer and KLM Cityhopper on behalf of their respective owners. Stobart Air has operating bases at Cork and Dublin for Aer Lingus Regional, at London Southend for Flybe, at London City Airport for BA CityFlyer and Amsterdam Airport for KLM Cityhopper.
