  Giusy Sciacca






Giusy Sciacca holds a degree in Modern Languages and Literature and she is completing her professional education in ‘Responsible Innovation’ at Delft University. An active air traffic controller based in Milan Linate Tower, she is also Secretary General of ANACNA, the Italian association of air traffic controllers and ATS personnel. Giusy is actively involved as IFATCA representative at the Technical Sub-Group (TSG) of the Industrial Consultation Body (ICB) and Chair of the IFATCA RTO group.
