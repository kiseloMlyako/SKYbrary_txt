Background
This safety enhancement is designed to reduce loss of control accidents by encouraging Part 121 air carriers to adopt consensus policies and procedures relating to mode awareness and energy state management, as appropriate to their respective operations.

Detailed Implementation Plan
Related Outputs
The Airline Transport Association (ATA)developed and distributed a report Mode Awareness and Energy State Management Aspects of Flight Deck Automation containing consensus policies and procedures relating to mode awareness and energy state management, as appropriate to their respective operations.
