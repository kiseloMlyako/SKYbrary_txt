Amsterdam Airport Schiphol is the Netherlands' main international airport, located 4.9 nm southwest of Amsterdam, in the municipality of Haarlemmermeer. The airport used to have the IATA code of SPL, which has fallen into disuse and has been replaced by AMS.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
