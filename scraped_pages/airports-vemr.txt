Description
Dimapur Airport is located 10 km southeast of the central business district of Dimapur, a city in the state of Nagaland, India. It is the only civil airport serving the state.
Climatology
Humid Sub-tropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - Hot humid summers and cooler winter. Significant precipitation in all seasons but drier in the winter.
