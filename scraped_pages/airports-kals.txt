Description
San Luis Valley Regional Airport, also known as Bergman Field, is a public use airport located 4 km south of the central business district of Alamosa, a city in Alamosa County, Colorado.
Climatology
Semi-arid Steppe climate (Köppen climate classification BSk) - Little precipitation, hot summers and cold winters, sometimes with large changes in temperature in a 24 hour period.
