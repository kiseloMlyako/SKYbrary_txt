Description
Lihue Airport is a state-owned public-use airport located in the Līhuʻe on the southeast coast of the island of Kauaʻi in Kauai County, Hawaiʻi, United States, 2 nm east of the center of Līhuʻe.
Climatology
Tropical Monsoon climate (Köppen climate classification Am) - monthly mean temperatures above 18°C in every month of the year and features wet and dry seasons. A tropical monsoon climate typically features less pronounced dry seasons than a tropical savanna climate and see less variance in temperatures during the course of the year.
