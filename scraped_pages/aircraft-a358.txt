The A350-800 is to seat 270 passengers in a three-class configuration with a 9-abreast seating, and have a range of 8,300 nm. It is designed to compete to directly replace the Airbus A330-200.
Note: The production of this aircraft was cancelled and consequently, the ICAO Designator A358 was from ICAO Doc 8643.
