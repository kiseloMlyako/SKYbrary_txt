  Renee Pylchen-Medwed






Renee Pylchen-Medwed works as a Human Performance Expert at Eurocontrol HQ in Brussels. She is a registered Aviation Psychologist with more than 15 years of practical experience in the aviation industry. She joined EUROCONTROL CRDS Budapest in 2003 where she worked as a validation and human factors specialist. Since 2010 she mainly provides human performance support to various operational and technical SESAR projects.
