Jean-Marc Flon











  Jean-Marc Flon






JEAN-MARC FLON is General Manager Air Traffic Services at Paris CDG where his responsibilities include oversight of Approach and Tower Control as well as Apron Management. Earlier in his career, he was a controller at Chambery, Paris Orly and Nice, during which time he was active in the French Air Traffic Controllers' Association including serving as President.
