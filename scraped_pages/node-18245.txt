Channel Express was an airline based in Bournemouth (EGHH), operatinf scheduled services from Bournemouth to the Channel Islands, as well as ad hoc charter. In 2006 the name was dropped and the company was rebranded as Jet2.com, the name given to the company's low cost airline.
