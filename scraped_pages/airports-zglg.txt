Description
Yongzhou Lingling Airport is an airport serving the city of Yongzhou in Hunan Province, China. It is located in Lengshuitan District, 8.8 km from the city center.
Climatology
Generally warm and humid summers with mild winters Köppen climate classification Cwa.. During the winter, the main weather feature is the mid-latitude cyclone. Convective thunderstorms provide precipitation in the summer months.
