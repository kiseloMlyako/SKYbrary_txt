Description
Morlaix - Ploujean Airport is an airport located 2 km northeast of Morlaix, a commune of the Finistère department in the Brittany region of France. The airport is located in the former commune of Ploujean.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
