Gerald R. Ford International Airport is a commercial airport located approximately 13 nm southeast of Grand Rapids, in Cascade Township, Michigan.
Humid Continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dfb) - Variable weather patterns and a large seasonal temperature variance. Summers are often warm and humid with frequent thunderstorms and winters can be very cold with frequent snowfall and persistent snow cover. Lake effect snowfall is frequent due to the proximity of Lake Michigan.
