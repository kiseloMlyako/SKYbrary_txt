Description
Comox Valley Airport is a military aerodrome located on the east side of Vancouver Island. It has a commercial terminal which serves the communities of Comox, Courtenay, Cumberland and the surrounding area.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter.
