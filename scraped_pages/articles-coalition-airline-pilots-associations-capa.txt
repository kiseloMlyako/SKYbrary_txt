The U.S.-based Coalition of Airline Pilots Associations (CAPA) is a trade association representing more than 35,000 professional passenger and cargo airline pilots. The organization's membership is comprised of unions representing pilots a American Airlines (Allied Pilots Association), UPS Airlines (Independent Pilots Association), NetJets (NetJets Association of Shared Aircraft Pilots), Atlas Air (Teamsters Local 2750), Horizon Air, ABX Air, Cape Air, Omni Air and Silver Airways (Teams Local 1224), and Republic Airlines (Teamsters Local 357). 
CAPA's mission is to address, safety, security, legislative and regulatory issues affecting professional flight deck crew members on matters of common interest to the individual member unions.
