Shizuoka Airport, also called Mt. Fuji Shizuoka Airport, is located in Shizuoka Prefecture Japan.
The airport is located in Makinohara and Shimada. It is 27 km southwest of Shizuoka Station and about 45 km from Hamamatsu, 80 km from Mt. Fuji, 130 km from Nagoya airport, and 175 km from Tokyo in a direct line.
Climatology
Humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa).
