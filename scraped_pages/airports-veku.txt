Description
Silchar Airport is a civil enclave airport sharing the facilities of Kumbhirgram Air Force Base and is located 25 km northeast of Silchar, a city in the state of Assam, India.
Climatology
Humid Sub-tropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - Hot humid summers and cooler winter. Significant precipitation in all seasons but drier in the winter.
