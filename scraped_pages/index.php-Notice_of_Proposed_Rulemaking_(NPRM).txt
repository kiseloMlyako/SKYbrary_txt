Definition
Used by an National Aviation Authority (NAA) to give notice of an intention to change their Regulatory regime for aircraft design, production, maintenance or operation and allow interested parties the opportunity to comment before actual changes are made. Equivalent to Notice of Proposed Action/Amendment (NPA).
