Metar







Enschede Airport Twente is located 2 nm from Enschede, Twente, Netherlands. The airport is currently uncontrolled and closed for all aviation except for local users, pending re-operation and redevelopment of the airport.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
