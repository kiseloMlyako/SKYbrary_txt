Lackland Air Force Base is a United States Air Force facility located in San Antonio, Texas. Lackland is part of Joint Base San Antonio, an amalgamation of the United States Army Fort Sam Houston, the United States Air Force Randolph Air Force Base and Lackland Air Force Base, which were merged on 1 October 2010.
