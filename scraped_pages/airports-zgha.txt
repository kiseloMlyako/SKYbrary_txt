Description
Changsha Huanghua International Airport is the airport serving Changsha, Hunan province, People's Republic of China, as well as nearby cities such as Zhuzhou and Xiangtan. Located about 20 km of the city centre in the town of Huanghua in Changsha County.
Climatology
Humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa.) Hot muggy summers with frequent thunderstorms. Winters are mild and precipitation during this season comes from mid-latitude cyclones. This climate type is quite common on the east coasts of continents.
