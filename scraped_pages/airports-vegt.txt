Description
Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, also known as Guwahati International Airport, is a joint use military civil airport located 20 km west of the city or Guwahati, in the state of Assam. It is the primary international airport of the North-Eastern States of India.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cwa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters.
