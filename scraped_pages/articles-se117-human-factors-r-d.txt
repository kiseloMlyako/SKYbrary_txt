Background
The purpose of this project is to conduct research into:

Correcting and eliminating the underlying reasons/causes for procedural noncompliance.
Fight crew overload.
Preventing plan continuation errors.
Developing an effective tactical decision making model for flight crews.
Developing human performance models.
