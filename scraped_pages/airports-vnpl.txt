Phaplu Airport is an airport serving Phaplu (also spelled Phaphlu), in Nepal. The bitumen surfaced runway 02/20 is 2230 feet/680 metres long and 65 feet / 20 metres wide and has no approach or runway lighting or navaids. All landings are on 20. ATS is FIS, only VFR flights are permitted and there is no RFFS.
