  Johan Glantz






Captain Johan Glantz FRAeS, is presently one of the two elected Executive Board Members Technical Affairs at the European Cockpit Association (ECA) with primary responsibility for ECA involvement in Air Traffic Management, Aerodrome, Single European Sky and Helicopter issues. He is also an active Training and Check Captain flying for Scandinavian Airlines (SAS). He has a degree in Human Factors / CRM from University West, Sweden and is presently perusing a MSc in Air Safety Management with City University, London.
