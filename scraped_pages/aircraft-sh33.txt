Regional airliner and utility freighter. In service since 1975. Improved, stretched development of SC-7 Skyvan. More powerful development 330-200. Various freighter versions were built including the Sherpa with a rear loading ramp. Military version C-23 for US Air Force and Army and 330-UT for Thailands Army.
