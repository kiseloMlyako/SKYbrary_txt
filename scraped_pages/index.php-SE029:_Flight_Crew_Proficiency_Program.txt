Air Line Pilots Association (ALPA), in collaboration with other Pilot Associations, developed a program to continuously improve pilot performance and proficiency using information from other programs such as FOQA (Flight Operation Quality Assurance), AQP (Advanced Qualification Program) and ASAP (Aviation Safety Action Program). The program is non-punitive and voluntary, and is managed by the individual airlines.Â Airlines for America (A4A), in collaboration with other Airline Groups (Aligned Carriers), voluntarily implemented the program.
