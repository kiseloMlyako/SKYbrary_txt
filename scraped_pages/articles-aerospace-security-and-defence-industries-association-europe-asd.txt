Description
The Aerospace, Security and Defence Industries Association of Europe (ASD) represents more than 3,000 companies in the European aerospace, defense and security industries. Its direct members include 22 major European companies and 23 national associations. 
ASD is a member of the International Coordinating Council of Aerospace Industries Associations (ICCAIA).
