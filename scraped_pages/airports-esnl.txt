Description
Lycksele Airport is an airport in Lycksele, Lapland, Sweden.
Climatology
Lycksele has Subarctic climate (Köppen climate classification Dfc) with short cool summers and long cold and snowy winters. Despite its extremely northern latitude, the climate is relatively mild compared to other places at similar latitude because of the Gulf Stream.
