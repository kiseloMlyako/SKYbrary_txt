Definition
A winter storm associated with Freezing Rain
Description
Freezing Rain usually occurs ahead of a Warm Front when precipitation falls through warm air (above freezing) into a cold air mass (air below freezing).
For further information on how freezing rain is formed and the hazards associated with it, see the main article Freezing Rain
