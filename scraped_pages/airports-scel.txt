Description
Santiago Arturo Merino Benítez International Airport, formerly known as Pudahuel Airport is situated 15 km north-west of downtown Santiago, Chile. It is a joint civil/military use airport.
Climatology
Dry-summer subtropical ”Mediterranean" climate (Köppen climate classification Csb) - characterized by warm, dry summers and mild winters.
