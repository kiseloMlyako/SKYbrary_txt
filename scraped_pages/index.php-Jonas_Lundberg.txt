Jonas Lundberg is a Professor in Human Centred Design at Linköping University, Norrköping, Sweden, and has a PhD in Computer Science from Linköping University, 2005, in the area of interaction design, with human-automation collaboration as his current main area of research. He has also conducted research in Safety Science since 2006.
