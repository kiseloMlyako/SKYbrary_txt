MK Airlines was a cargo airline registered in the United Kingdom, providing worldwide freight operations. Its main base was Ostend-Bruges International Airport, Belgium, with hubs at Luxembourg-Findel International Airport, Kent International Airport, Kotoka International Airport, Accra, Ghana and OR Tambo International Airport, Johannesburg.
