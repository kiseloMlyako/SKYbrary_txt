Light aircraft. In service since the early 80s. Member of SOCATA's Caribbean TB single engine aircraft family. More powerful, retractable gear version of TB-10 Tobago. TB-21 Trinidad TC is a turbocharged version.
Note: The ICAO designator TRIN was deleted from ICAO Doc 8643 and replaced by TB20 and TB21 in 2014.
