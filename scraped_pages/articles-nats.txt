Description
NATS (formerly National Air Traffic Services and now NATS Holdings Limited) provides air traffic control services at 15 of the UK's biggest airports and manages all UK upper airspace (London FIR, Scottish FIR and Shanwick Oceanic). NATS also provides air traffic services and consultancy in over 30 countries worldwide.
