Andrés Martín Asuero PhD, is a mindfulness certified trainer in MBSR with more than 15 years of experience in teaching and research investigating stress reduction among health professionals. He has published three books and 10 scientific studies on mindfulness and wellbeing. He is director of Instituto esMindfulness in Barcelona.
