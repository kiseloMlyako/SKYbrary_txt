Long range airliner and freighter. In service since 1959. Built as DC-8- 10, 20, 30, 40 and 50. DC-8-10 initial domestic version, DC-8-20 with more powerful engines. Intercontinental series - 30, - 40 and - 50 with shorter fuselage and more powerful engines. Only DC-8-50 remain in service as freighter. The DC85 is member of the DC-8 family of aircraft.
