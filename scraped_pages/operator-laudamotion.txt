Lauda, legally Laudamotion GmbH (formerly Amira Air), is an Austrian low-cost airline based in the Concorde Business Park in Schwechat, near Vienna, Austria. It is from 2018 a subsidiary of Ryanair Holdings, and a sister airline to Ryanair DAC, Ryanair UK, Malta Air and Buzz.
