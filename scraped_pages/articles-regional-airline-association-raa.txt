Article Information

Category:












The Regional Airline Association (RAA) is a member-based organization that advocates for regional airlines in North America. RAA describes itself as providing “a unified voice of advocacy for North American regional airlines aimed at promoting a safe, reliable and strong regional airline industry.”
In the United States, where regional carriers often operate under code sharing agreements with larger carriers, connecting larger airports with smaller ones, regional airlines provide about 41% of scheduled passenger flights. They also provide the only scheduled air service to 67% of U.S. airports.
