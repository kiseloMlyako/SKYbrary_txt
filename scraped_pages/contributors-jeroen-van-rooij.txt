  Jeroen Van Rooij






Jeroen van Rooij is a Boeing 737 Captain with KLM. He worked in the training department for more than a decade, as Senior Instructor and head of the UPRT (Upset Prevention & Recovery Training) SME group. Currently he works at the safety department as an accident/incident investigator. He also holds a part-time position at NLR and is involved in human factors and training research. He has a Master’s degree in Philosophy.
