Description
The Asian Business Aviation Association (AsBAA) is a non-profit organization that represents business and general aviation (GA) entities based in Asia. Members include operators, manufacturers, service providers, finance and insurance providers. The organization provides a platform for communication and action related to issues such as safety, airport access, regulation, policy, professional development and training.
AsBAA, which is based in Hong Kong, is a member of the International Business Aviation Council (IBAC) and is affiliated with the National Business Aviation Association in the United States and the Royal Aeronautical Society.
