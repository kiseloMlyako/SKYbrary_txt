Description
Pellston Regional Airport of Emmet County, also known as Pellston Regional Airport, is a public airport located 2 km northwest of the central business district of Pellston, a village in Emmet County, Michigan.
Climatology
Humid Continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dfb) - Variable weather patterns and a large seasonal temperature variance. Summers are often warm and humid with frequent thunderstorms and winters can be very cold with frequent snowfall and persistent snow cover. Pellston recorded the state of Michigan's record low temperature, a frigid −53 °F (−47 °C). in 1933, and every winter is regularly called out in national weather reports as one of the coldest spots in the nation.
