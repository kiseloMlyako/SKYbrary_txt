Pakistan International Airlines Corporation, commonly known as PIA, is the flag carrier airline of Pakistan. The airline has its head office on the grounds of Jinnah International Airport in Karachi. Its main bases are at Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad/Rawalpindi.
