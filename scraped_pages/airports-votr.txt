Description
Tiruchirappalli International Airport, also referred to as Trichy International Airport, is a public airport located 5 km south of the central business district of Tiruchirapalli, a city in the state of Tamil Nadu, India.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification Aw) - monthly mean temperatures above 18°C, and a pronounced dry season. A Tropical Savanna climate has more pronounced dry seasons than a Tropical Monsoon climate.
