Background
The purpose of this Safety Enhancement is to outline research to be conducted by the aviation community (government, industry and academia) to enable development, implementation, and certification of on-board aircraft system technologies to assess airplane braking action and provide the data in real time to the pilot, other aircraft crews, air traffic controllers, and the airport operators.

Related Outputs
Research on on-aircraft runway friction measurement and reporting has been completed. The final report is expected to be released in early 2018.
