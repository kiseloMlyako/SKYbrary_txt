Medium-size corporate jet. In service since 1983 (Citation 7 since 1992). Designed as a high performance, mid-size long range corporate jet to supplement the Citation 1 and Citation 2, with swept super critical wing, new turbofans, T-tail and a new stretched fuselage. Citation 6 low cost derived from Citation 3. Citation 7 new improved version with more powerful engines and higher performance. Citation 7: MTOW 10,183 kg.
