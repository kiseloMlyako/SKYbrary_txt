Description
The International Air Cargo Assocation (TIACA) represents all parts of the air cargo industry, including airlines, airports, ground handlers, shippers, freight forwarders, and manufacturers.
The organization's self-stated vision is "a safe, profitable and united air cargo industry that embraces modern technologies and practices to sustainably and fairly serve trade and social development worldwide."
