Manston Airport is a closed British airport. It was branded as Manston, Kent International Airport and is located in the parish of Minster-in-Thanet and partly adjacent to the village of Manston in the Thanet district of Kent, England, 11 NM north-east of Canterbury. Formerly the site of RAF Manston, it was also briefly known as London Manston Airport.
