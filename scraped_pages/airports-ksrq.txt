Description
Sarasota–Bradenton International Airport is a public use airport in Sarasota County (terminal) and Manatee County (airfield). It is located 6 km north of the central business district of Sarasota and 9 km south of Bradenton.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Significant amounts of precipitation occur in all seasons in most areas.
