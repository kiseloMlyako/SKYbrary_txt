  Anders Ellerstrand






Anders Ellerstrand is presently an ATM Consultant, supporting EUROCONTROL in Brussels. Before retirement from Swedish LFV, he was an ATCO and a Watch Supervisor. His background also includes work for ICAO in African countries, safety work for LFV and an MSc Human Factors in Aviation with Coventry University.
