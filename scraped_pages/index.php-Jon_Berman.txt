  Jon Berman






Jon Berman is a Director of Greenstreet Berman Ltd, which is a Human Factors Consultancy operating across the high-hazard industries. His 40 years experience started in the aviation industry, before he joined the UK nuclear industry a few months before the accident at Chernobyl. Jon is a Chartered Ergonomist and Human Factors Specialist, a Chartered Psychologist, and both a Fellow and Past President of the Chartered Institute of Ergonomics and Human Factors (CIEHF).
