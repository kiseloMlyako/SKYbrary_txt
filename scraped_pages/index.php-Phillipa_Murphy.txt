Philippa Murphy is a Principal Human Factors Specialist working for RSSB. She has spent the majority of her career in the rail sector working on various human factors and behavioural safety projects and research for Railtrack, Network Rail and as a freelance consultant. Her areas of expertise at RSSB are in Non-Technical Skills training, development and integration, accident investigation and improving operational decision making.
