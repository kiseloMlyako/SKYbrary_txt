  Neville Stanton






Professor Neville Stanton is Professor of Human Factors in Transport within Engineering and Physical Sciences at the University of Southampton and conducts research into human performance in technological systems. In 2007 The Royal Aeronautical Society awarded him the Hodgson Medal and Bronze Award with colleagues for their work on flight-deck safety. Neville is the Principal Investigator for the Open Flight Deck Project.
