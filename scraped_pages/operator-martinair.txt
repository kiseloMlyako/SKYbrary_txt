Martinair is an airline headquartered on the grounds of Amsterdam Airport Schiphol, the Netherlands. It operates passenger and cargo services to over 50 destinations worldwide. Services are largely on a scheduled basis, but charter services are also operated. Its main base is Amsterdam Schiphol Airport.
