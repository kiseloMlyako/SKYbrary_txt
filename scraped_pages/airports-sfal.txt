Port Stanley Airport is a STOL port in the Falkland Islands, two miles outside the capital, Stanley. The airport is the only civil-operated airport in the islands with a paved runway. The airport is operated by the Government of the Falkland Islands, and is only used for internal flights between the Islands. International flights use Mount Pleasant Airport which is operated by the Royal Air Force.
