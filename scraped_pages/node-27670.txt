Description
The Interstate Aviation Committee (IAC) or Межгосударственный авиационный комитет (MAK) is an executive body overseeing the use and management of civil aviation in the Commonwealth of Independent States. Its responsibilities include accident investigation in Member States. It was established in 1991 when twelve of the former Soviet Union signed the Intergovernmental Agreement on Civil Aviation and Use of Airspace. In 2013, membership is stated to be Azerbaijan, Armenia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Moldova, the Russian Federation, Tadjikistan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan and Ukraine. The Committee is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
