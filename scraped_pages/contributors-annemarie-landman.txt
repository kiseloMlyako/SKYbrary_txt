  Annemarie Landman






Dr Annemarie Landman works at TNO, the Netherlands, as a researcher of cognitive performance in aviation and military operations. She also works part-time as a teacher at the Control and Operations section of the Delft University of Technology, where she graduated on the topic of startle and surprise. Her topics of interest include complex cognition under pressure, spatial disorientation, and training.
