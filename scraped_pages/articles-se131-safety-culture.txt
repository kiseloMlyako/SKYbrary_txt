Background
This safety enhancement reduces accidents and incidents by encouraging a culture that enhances operational safety. A safety culture can be enhanced by a safety management system that includes:

Development of an accident/incident cost analysis tool;
A self-audit process;
Risk management programs;
Revised standards for the Director of Safety (DOS); and
Development of an incident reporting and quality assurance system.
