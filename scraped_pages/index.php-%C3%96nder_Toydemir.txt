Önder Toydemir











  Önder Toydemir






Önder Toydemir is Air Traffic Manager of Turkish Air Traffic Control Centre. He graduated from Anadolu University Faculty of Aeronautics and Astronautics in Eskisehir in 1994 and attended General Directorate of State Airports Authority (DHMI) in the same year. He worked as approach and area controller, OJTI and shift supervisor for nineteen years. Between 2008 - 2013, he was the manager of TATCA Ankara Branch. He has been working as the Air Traffic Manager since 2013 in Turkish Air Traffic Control Centre (SMART Project).
