Peter Hudec became an ATCO in 1983 and an executive supervisor in 2005. He finished his TWR/APP and supervisor operational post at Bratislava, Slovakia at the end of March 2015. Since 1995 he has been working in various positions related to safety investigation and supervision and currently works for the ANSP Safety Division as a Safety Specialist.
