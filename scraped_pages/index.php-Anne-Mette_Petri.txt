  Anne-Mette Petri






Anne-Mette Petri is a Development & Training Specialist at Entry Point North. She started her career as a commercial pilot in 2000 on the B737. In 2007, Air Traffic Control caught her interest and she began working with both initial and development training of ATCOs, AFISOs and ATSEPs. She has studied Human Factors and System Safety at Lund University and today her main focus is within the Safety Training domain.
