AC120-66B
Advisory Circular AC120-66B (Aviation Safety Action Program (ASAP)) provides guidance for establishing an air transportation Aviation Safety Action Program (ASAP). The objective of the ASAP is to encourage air carrier and repair station employees to voluntarily report safety information that may be critical to identifying potential precursors to accidents.
