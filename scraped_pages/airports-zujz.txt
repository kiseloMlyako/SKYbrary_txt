Description
Jiuzhai Huanglong Airport is an airport in Songpan, Sichuan, People’s Republic of China. This airport serves two major scenic places of interest in this area, namely Huanglong (53 km away) and Jiuzhaigou (88 km away). It is 3447.65 m above sea level and is the airport with the 3rd highest altitude in the People's Republic of China (after Lhasa and Qamdo airports). The EMAS installation there was the first outside the USA.
