Adrian Woolrich-Burt











  Adrian Woolrich-Burt






Adrian Woolrich- Burt is a RNLI Lifeboat Commander on the River Thames. Prior to this he flew as a B737-800 Captain with Jet2.com, and taught theoretical knowledge on the B737MAX for Boeing Flight Services. He holds MSc degrees from the University of London, and a Post Graduate Diploma from the London School of Economics.
