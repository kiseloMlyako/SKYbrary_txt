Description
Salina Regional Airport is a public use airport located 6 km southwest of the central business district of Salina, a city in Saline County, Kansas.
Climatology
Salina lies in the transition zone between North America's humid subtropical climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) and humid continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dfa) and experiences hot, humid summers and cold, dry winters.
