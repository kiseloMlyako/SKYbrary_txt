Description
Hulunbuir Hailar Airport is an airport serving Hailar District of Hulunbuir, a prefecture-level city of Inner Mongolia, China.
Climatology
Dry arid climate common in middle latitude deserts (Koppen climate classification BWk). Annual average temperature is below 18°C (64°F). Daily minimum temperatures can frequently go below 0°C (32°F) during the winter season.
