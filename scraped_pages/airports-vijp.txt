Description
Jaipur Airport is an international airport located 13 km south of the central business district of Jaipur, the capital of the state of Rajasthan, Indian.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Most precipitation occurs during the monsoon season between late June and late September. Most summer rainfall occurs during thunderstorms. During winter, overnight radiation fog is often a problem.
