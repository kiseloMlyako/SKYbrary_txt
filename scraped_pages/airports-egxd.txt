Description
Former RAF and AAC flying station, Dishforth Airfield is now a British Army logistics base and is scheduled for closure in 2031.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter with a temperature range of only 14°C. Prevailing south-westerly winds from the Atlantic Ocean.
