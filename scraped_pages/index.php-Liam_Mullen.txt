Liam Mullen is a London-based consultant within the Intelligent Mobility and Smart Technologies (IMST) team of Atkins’ transportation business. He is an Aerospace Engineering graduate of the University of Sheffield and holds a Private Pilot’s Licence. Liam is involved in helping shape future strategy for the next generation of roads and is passionate about the power of disruptive technologies.
