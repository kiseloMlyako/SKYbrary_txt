  Leonie Boskeljon-Horst






Leonie Boskeljon- Horst is a senior aviation psychologist and human factors specialist and currently the head of occurrence investigation of the RNLAF, focussing on implementing the Safety-II perspective through reactive and proactive occurrence investigations, research projects and safety promotion. Leonie is currently finishing her PhD thesis on safety culture and crew resource management training.
