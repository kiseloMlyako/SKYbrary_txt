The International Council of Air Shows (ICAS) was founded in 1967 by air show industry professionals to protect and promote air show interests in North America. The organization promotes the need for standardized industry practices in areas such as safety, insurance and in how the air show industry interacts with government and military officials.
To achieve its goal of building and sustaining a vibrant air show industry, ICAO "demands that its members operating their business at only the highest levels of safety, professionals and integrity."
More information is available on the ICAS website.
