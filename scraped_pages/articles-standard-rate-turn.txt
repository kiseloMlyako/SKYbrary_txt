Standard Rate Turn
Rate One Turn
Definition
A turn rate of 3 degrees per second.
Description
An aircraft conducting a Standard Rate Turn will turn a full circle in 2 minutes. A holding pattern with 1 minute straight legs therefore takes 4 minutes to complete.
