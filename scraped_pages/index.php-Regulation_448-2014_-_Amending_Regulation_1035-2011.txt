Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) No 448/2014 of 2 May 2014 amending Implementing Regulation (EU) No 1035/2011 by updating references to the Annexes to the Chicago Convention - text published in the Official Journal of the European Union 
Objective
To ammend the Annexes to Implementing Regulation (EU) No 1035/2011 in accordance with the changes to ICAO provisions.
Summary
ICAO amended Annexes 3, 4, 10, 11, 14 and 15 to the Chicago Convention recently before this regulation. This Regulation updates the References to the Convention contained in Implementing Regulation (EU) No 1035/2011.
The updates are listed in the Annex to the Regulation.
Entry Into Force
This Regulation entered into force in May 2014.
