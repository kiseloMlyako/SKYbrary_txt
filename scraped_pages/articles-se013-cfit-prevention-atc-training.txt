Background
This safety enhancement reinforces current safety alert procedures and good air traffic operating practices by providing initial and recurrent ATC CFIT training for all controllers.
Through the use of Air Traffic Bulletins (ATBs), Air Traffic Controllers now receive required initial and annual recurrent ATC CFIT training.
