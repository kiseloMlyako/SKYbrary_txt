  Tom Laursen






Tom Laursen is an Air Traffic Controller (ATCO) and has worked with three different Air Navigation Service Providers in Denmark, Bahrain, and Switzerland. During his career as an ATCO he held different positions, and has participated in different projects, building in-depth knowledge within safety and investigations. Tom has a Master’s Degree in Human Factors in Aviation from Linköping University, Sweden.
