Sky Regional Airlines (SRA) was an airline whose corporate headquarters was located at Toronto Pearson International Airport, Ontario, Canada. Linked with Skyservice Business Aviation, it began operation under the Air Canada Express brand on 1 May 2011. On 1 March 2021, Air Canada announced that the airline was going to consolidate all regional flying under the Jazz banner thereby ending the affiliation between Sky Regional and Air Canada. Sky Regional operated its last flight on 30 March 2021.
