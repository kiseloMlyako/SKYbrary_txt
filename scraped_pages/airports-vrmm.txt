Velana International Airport, also known as Malé International Airport, previously known as Ibrahim Nasir International Airport, is the main international airport in the Maldives. It is located on Hulhulé Island in the North Malé Atoll, nearby the capital island Malé.
The Waterdrome, where numerous seaplanes operate from, is located to the east of the main airport complex. Approach and departure routes from the waterdrome cross the main runway.
