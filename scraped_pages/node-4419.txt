Description
Courchevel Airport is a French airport that serves Courchevel, a ski area. The airport has a very short runway of only 525 m with an average gradient of 18.5%. The airport's elevation is at 2008 m.

The longitudinal profile of the Courchevel runway (altitudes are in feet amsl).
Climatology
Humid Continental climate (Köppen climate classification Dfb) - Variable weather patterns and a large seasonal temperature variance. Summers are often warm and humid with frequent thunderstorms and winters can be very cold with frequent snowfall and persistent snow cover.
