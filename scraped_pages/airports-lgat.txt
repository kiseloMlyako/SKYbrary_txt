Description
Ellinikon International Airport, sometimes spelled Hellinikon was the international airport of Athens for sixty years up until 2001 when it was replaced by Eleftherios Venizelos International Airport. It is located 7 km south of Athens, and just west of Glyfada.
Climatology
Mediterranean climate (Köppen climate classification Csa) - Hot, dry summer and a mild wet winter.
