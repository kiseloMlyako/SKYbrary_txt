Air Ground Communication



Description
"The passage of voice and/or data between an aircraft and a ground station such as air traffic control or aircraft operating agency."
ICAO Annex 10 – Aeronautical Communications defines air ground communication as “Two-way communication between aircraft and stations or locations on the surface of the earth.”
For further information read the lead article about Air Ground Communication.
