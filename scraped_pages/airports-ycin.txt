RAAF Base Curtin, also sometimes RAAF Curtin is a joint use RAAF military air base and civilian airport located 19 nm (35 km; 22 mi) southeast of the town of Derby on the north coast of Western Australia. No RAAF units are permanently based at Curtin and it is maintained by a small caretaker staff during peacetime.
