  Kathryn Jones






Kathryn Jones leads the CAA’s Human Factors Programme. She is a specialist in fatigue management and has a broad range of regulatory HF experience. She is a member of the ICAO Fatigue Management and Human Performance task forces and holds a Master degree in Air Safety Management. She is currently focused on how systems thinking methods can improve our understanding of the influences on people and to support them to their best.
