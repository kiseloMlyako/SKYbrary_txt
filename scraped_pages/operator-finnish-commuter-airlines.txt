Finnish Commuter Airlines Oy, trading as Finncomm Airlines, is a regional airline with its head office on the grounds of Seinäjoki Airport in Ilmajoki, Finland, near Seinäjoki. The carrier operates from its base at Helsinki-Vantaa Airport.
