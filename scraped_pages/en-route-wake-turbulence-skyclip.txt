Philip Marien

Philip Marien started as an en-route controller in Maastricht UAC over 30 years ago. Ten years later, he started doing incident investigations, which he has now been doing full time for the last 15 years. In addition, he has been involved in the professional association of ATCO in Maastricht and from there, in the international federation IFATCA, where he volunteers as editor and communications coordinator.

Catalin Lepadatu

A graduate of the Faculty of Aerospace Engineering - University POLITEHNICA of Bucharest, Catalin Lepadatu worked for the Romanian ANSP (ROMATSA) for 10 years, as an licensed ATCO and then as the Head of the Technical System Development Division. He joined EUROCONTROL in 2001 as an ATM/ATC Expert and has worked for different domains since: data link, fast time simulations, wake turbulence re-categorisation and airspace management. Between 2008-2011, he was the FAA-EUROCONTROL co-chairman of the RECAT project working group.
