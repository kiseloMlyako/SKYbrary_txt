Air Nostrum, Líneas Aéreas del Mediterráneo, S.A., also known as Iberia Regional, is a regional airline based in Valencia, Spain. Air Nostrum is an independent carrier which operates as a franchisee of Iberia Airlines. Its main base is Valencia Airport, with hubs at Barcelona Airport and Madrid-Barajas Airport.
