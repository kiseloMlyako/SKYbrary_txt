  Erick Hoarau






Erick Hoarau occupies a position of First Officer with a French airline. He has been a CRM Trainer for 10 years and was nominated CRM Trainer Examiner in 2016. In 2015 he obtained a Diploma in ‘Human Factors for the Conception of Human-Machine Systems’ at the Paris V René Descartes University. He is a member of a focus group gathering pilots and controllers to address flight safety through human factors.
