Description
Military training base, 15 nm south west of Swindon, UK. For many years home to the RAF tactical transport force.
Climatology
Temperate Marine climate/Oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfb). Moderately cool summer and comparatively warm winter.
Prevailing wind from the South West. Susceptible to fog in December if a high pressure system becomes static over the UK.
