Description
Nantucket Memorial Airport is a public airport located on the south side of the island of Nantucket 5 km southeast of the town of Nantucket, Massachusetts.
Climatology
Humid continental climate with a strong maritime influence (Köppen climate classification Dfb/Cfb) Precipitation amounts are fairly consistent from month to month regardless of the time of year. Due to the strong oceanic influence, summers on Natucket are cooler and winters are warmer than on the mainland.
