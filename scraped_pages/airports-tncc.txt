Description
Hato International Airport or Curaçao International Airport (originally named Dr. Albert Plesman International Airport) is the airport of Willemstad, Curaçao, Netherlands Antilles. It services to the Caribbean region, South America, North America and Europe.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification Aw), rarely affected directly by tropical storms as it is on the southern fringe of the Atlantic/Caribbean Hurricane Belt.
