Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, also known as Hyderabad International Airport, is an international airport located near Shamshabad, 22 km south of the central business district of Hyderabad, a city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification Aw) - monthly mean temperatures above 18°C, and a pronounced dry season. A Tropical Savanna climate has more pronounced dry seasons than a Tropical Monsoon climate.
