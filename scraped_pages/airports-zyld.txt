Description
Yichun Lindu Airport is an airport serving the city of Yichun in China's northeastern Heilongjiang Province. It is located in a forest approximately 9 km from downtown Yichun.
Climatology
Within the Köppen Climate Classification System, (Koppen climate classification Dwb) moist continental mid-latitude climates have warm to cool summers and cold winters. The location of this Köppen climate type is pole ward of the C climates. The average temperature of the warmest month is greater than 10°C (50°F), while the coldest month is less than -3°C (27°F). Winters are severe with snowstorms, strong winds, and bitter cold from Continental Polar or Arctic air masses. Average temperature of the coldest month is -3°C (27°F) or lower. Average temperature of the warmest month is greater than 10°C (50°F). Precipitation is equally spread across the year.
