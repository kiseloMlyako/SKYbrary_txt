The Eclipse 500 is a small six-seat business jet aircraft manufactured by Eclipse Aviation. The model 500 became the first of a new class of Very Light Jet when it was delivered in late 2006. The aircraft is powered by two lightweight Pratt & Whitney Canada PW610F turbofan engines in aft fuselage-mounted nacelles.
