Alitalia (Compagnia Aerea Italiana S.p.A.) used to be the flag carrier and national airline of Italy. The company had its head office in Fiumicino, Italy. Its main hub was Leonardo da Vinci-Fiumicino Airport, Rome. Alitalia was Italy's biggest airline.
