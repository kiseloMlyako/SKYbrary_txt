  Fabrizio Bracco






Fabrizio Bracco is Associate Professor in Psychology of Work and Organisations at the University of Genoa. As an expert in cognitive ergonomics, he has carried out numerous interventions for risk management and for the promotion of non-technical skills. On the organisational side, he conducts research and consultancy in the field of safety culture and organisational resilience, event analysis methods and no-blame culture.
