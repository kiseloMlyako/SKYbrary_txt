Anna Vereker is a Chartered Occupational Psychologist and EAAP Aviation Psychologist. She has a broad range of experience with aviation human factors, including with the military and air traffic control. She is passionate about encouraging robust consideration of human factors issues in complex systems, and continues to be curious about new methods, research, and ways to influence the industry.
