Pinnacle Airlines, Inc. (formerly Express Airlines I) is an American regional airline based in Memphis, Tennessee, operating Delta Connection for Delta Air Lines. Its main base is Memphis International Airport, with hubs at Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport, Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport and Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport.
