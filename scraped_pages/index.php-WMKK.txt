Kuala Lumpur International Airport (commonly known as KLIA) is one of Southeast Asia's major aviation hubs. It is also Malaysia's main international airport. It is situated in the Sepang district, in the south of the state of Selangor, about 50 km from the capital city, Kuala Lumpur.
