8.33 kHz above FL195 in the ICAO EUR Region

The attached notice provides brief information on 8.33 kHz above FL195 in the ICAO EUR Region which takes effect 15 March 2007.


The widespread distribution and display of this information notice - especially to flight crew, flight dispatchers and air traffic controllers - is strongly encouraged.


Paper copies of the information notice are available free of charge on request from: [email protected]


For further information regarding the 8.33 kHz channel spacing:

access the 8.33 kHz web based training package: http://elearning.eurocontrol.int/IANS/ATC/ATCR/ETT_verticalexpansion/wsVerticalExpansion/intro.html
visit the 8.33 kHz website: http://www.eurocontrol.int/833
