Hawaiian Airlines, Inc. is a commercial airline in the United States. It is the largest airline based in the State of Hawai'i. Hawaiian Airlines, based in Honolulu, operates its main hub at Honolulu International Airport and also operates a secondary hub out of Kahului Airport on the island of Maui.
