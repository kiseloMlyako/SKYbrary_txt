The Swearingen Merlin or the Fairchild Aerospace Merlin is a pressurised, twin turboprop business aircraft first produced by Swearingen Aircraft, and later by Fairchild at a plant in San Antonio, Texas. The Merlin II was developed from the Beech Queen air and a total of 120 aircraft were build. The aircraft first flew in April 1965.
