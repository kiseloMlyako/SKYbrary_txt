Thomsonfly was a British non-scheduled service airline which began operations in 2004 and was amalgamated from 2005 with Britannia Airways. The new combined operation took the Thomsonfly name. From the winter of 2008/2009, it was merged with First Choice Airways to create Thomson Airways, part of the TUI Travel Group.
