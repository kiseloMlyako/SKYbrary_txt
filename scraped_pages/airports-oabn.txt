Description
Bamyan Airport serves the city of Bamyan in central Afghanistan.
Climatology
Bamyan experiences a cold desert/semi arid climate (Köppen climate classification BWk and BSk) with short cold winters and very warm dry summers. Most rain falls in the spring.
