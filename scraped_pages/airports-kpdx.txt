Description
Portland International Airport is located on the south side of the Columbia River within the current city limits of Portland in Multnomah County, six miles by air northeast of downtown Portland. Portland International Airport is often referred to by its IATA airport code, PDX.
Climatology
Oceanic/Dry-summer subtropical ”Mediterranean" climate (Köppen climate classification Csb) - characterized by warm, dry summers and mild winters.
