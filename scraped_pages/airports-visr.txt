Description
Srinagar Airport is an international airport located 10 km southwest of Srinagar, a city in the state of Jammu and Kashmir, India.
Climatology
Humid “subtropical” climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) - characterized by hot, humid summers and cool winters. Most precipitation occurs during the monsoon season between late June and late September. Most summer rainfall occurs during thunderstorms.
