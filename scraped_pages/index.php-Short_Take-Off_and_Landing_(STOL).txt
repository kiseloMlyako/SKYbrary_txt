Article Information

Category:

STOL is an acronym for short take-off and landing, a term used to describe aircraft with very short runway requirements. Aircraft with this capability include:

ANTONOV An-72
CASA C-212 Aviocar
DE HAVILLAND CANADA DHC-7
DE HAVILLAND CANADA DHC-6
DE HAVILLAND CANADA DHC-2
PILATUS Chiricahua
FAIRCHILD DORNIER 228
BRITISH AEROSPACE BAe-146-100
SUKHOI Su-80
