  Robert Mauro






Dr. Robert Mauro is a Senior Research Scientist at Decision Research and an Associate Professor of Psychology at the University of Oregon. He works on automation, pilot and space flight controller training, flightdeck procedures, pilot decision-making, and risk assessment. He has worked with regulators and major airlines in the USA and Europe, and his work has been supported, among others, by NASA, the FAA, and the National Science Foundation.
