Wuxi Airport, which is officially called Sunan Shuofang International Airport,(IATA: WUX) is an airport serving the cities of Wuxi and Suzhou in southern Jiangsu Province, China. It is located in Shuofang, which is 12 km southeast of Wuxi and 22 km northwest of Suzhou. ARP elevation of Wuxi is 5 metres.
