Definition
An item whose failure or malfunction could have one or more of the following effects or characteristics:

affect safety on the ground or in flight
be undetectable during operations
have significant operational economic impact
have significant non-operational economic impact.

It is sometimes referred to as a Maintenance Significant Item (MSI)
