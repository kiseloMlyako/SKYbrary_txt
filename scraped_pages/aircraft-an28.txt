Commuter airliner and utility transport aircraft. In limited service since 1986. Light passenger and utility transport development for short haul routes based on the An-14. The production was transferred to Polands PZL Mielec in 1978. Used by Polish Air Force and Navy. Westernised version M-28 Skytruck has been exported by Poland since 1996. New stretched, more powerful development of Antonov is the An-38.
