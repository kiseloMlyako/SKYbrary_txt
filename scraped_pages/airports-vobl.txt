Description
Bengaluru International Airport is an international airport serving the Indian city of Bangalore and is located 4 km south of Devanahalli and is 40 km from the central business district of Bangalore, in the state of Karnataka, India.
Climatology
Tropical Savanna climate (Köppen climate classification Aw) - monthly mean temperatures above 18°C, and a pronounced dry season. A Tropical Savanna climate has more pronounced dry seasons than a Tropical Monsoon climate.
