  Suzette Woodward






Dr Suzette Woodward is a former paediatric intensive care nurse who has worked for over 40 years in the UK National Health Service. For 20 of those years, she led national patient safety programmes. She was Patient Safety Director at the National Patient Safety Agency and National Campaign Director for ‘Sign up to Safety’. She is the author of three books on patient safety.
